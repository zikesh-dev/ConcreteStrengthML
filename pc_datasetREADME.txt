Columns Description

SL No.: Serial number for each data entry.
Water (kg/m³): Amount of water used per cubic meter of concrete.
Cement (kg/m³): Amount of cement used per cubic meter of concrete.
Fine Aggregate (kg/m³): Amount of fine aggregate (sand) used per cubic meter of concrete.
Coarse Aggregate (kg/m³): Amount of coarse aggregate (gravel or crushed stone) used per cubic meter of concrete.
Hearthstone (kg/m³): Amount of hearthstone (a type of additive) used per cubic meter of concrete.
Fly Ash (kg/m³): Amount of fly ash (a supplementary cementitious material) used per cubic meter of concrete.
Super Plasticizer (kg/m³): Amount of superplasticizer (a chemical admixture) used per cubic meter of concrete.
Actual Concrete Strength (MPa): Measured compressive strength of the concrete in megapascals (MPa).
